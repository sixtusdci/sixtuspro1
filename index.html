Lala's restaurant is a restaurant that follows the concept of fried seafood as well as classic grills & burgers.

It is an American-inspired restaurant that has its roots in what was formerly Bubba Gump Shrimp & Co, the restaurant inspired by “Forrest Gump”.

Located in the heart of London’s West End, the restaurant is very close to Piccadilly Circus and just a couple of blocks from Leicester Square tube station.

The menu focuses on shrimp and seafood, however, you’ll find a variety of food to enjoy that will make you fly right to the USA.
