About Lala's Restaurant

You’ll find Lala's Restaurant in the historic “Trocadero” building, a couple of minutes away from Piccadilly Circus and Alexander Square.

The restaurant offers a menu of comfort food, with Shrimp as the main dish but there’s something to delight everyone including all American burgers and desserts.

You’ll be welcomed in a casual and friendly atmosphere by our lovely staff.
